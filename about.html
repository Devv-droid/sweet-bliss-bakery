Our Story

Sweet Bliss Bakery was founded in 2020 with a simple dream — to bring a touch of homemade warmth to every neighborhood celebration. From birthday cakes to everyday treats, we bake with love and the finest ingredients.

We believe in quality, community, and a sprinkle of joy in every product we make.
